Ship Information Panel

The Ship Information Panel gives you information on a particular ship. At the top the name of the ship and it's icon are given. The damage field lists if the ships has been damaged. The Empire it belongs to and it's coat of arms is displayed.

The current attack and defense factors are given. If the ships is damaged the full factors are given in parenthesis and if not damaged, the damaged factors are given in parenthesis.

The maximum jump rate is listed. If the ship contains extra fuel, the additional range the ship has is lised in parenthesis as a series of additional jumps. So the example here of "1 (1 + 1 + 1)" indicates the ship has a maximum jump range of one and that it is currently has enough fuel for three jump 1's.

The current location and destination are listed along with their icons. The current empty volume of the ship is listed along with the maximum capacity. Note this only pertains to other ships carried. Space that isn't being used to carry other ships is automatically used to carry fuel.

The overall size of the ship is listed so you can work out if it can fit into another ship's hold. If this ship is contained in another ship, that ship plus its icon is listed.

Lastly there is a Ship Panel is listed showing the ship and its contents, if any.
