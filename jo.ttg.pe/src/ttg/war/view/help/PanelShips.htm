Ships Panel

The Ships Panel shows you a selection of ships. Their icons appear in a tree view with ships that are carried by other ships displayed as children in the tree. Double clicking on any ships will change the Information Panel to display details on that ship.

A ship may be docked to one another by selecting the two of them and clicking on the "dock" button. The smaller ship will then be docked within the larger ship, if it has sufficient capacity.

A ship that is currently docked within another ship may be undocked from it. Select the ship to undock and click the "undock" button. The smaller ship then appear on the display alongside the rest of the ships.

You can also transfer fuel between two ships from a Ship Panel. Select the two ships click the "fuel" button. The Fuel Transfer Dialog will appear and you can start the exchange.
