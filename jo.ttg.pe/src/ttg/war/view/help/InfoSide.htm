Pocket Empire Information Panel

The Pocket Empire Information Panel gives you information on how one side is doing in the game. At the top the name and coat of arms of the side is given. The total accumulated resources in the current stockpile is listed, the total number of people in the pocket empire, and the maximum technological level present in the empire. Statistics on the empire's fleet is given in terms of cumulative attack factors, cumulative defense factors and the total value of the fleet. The current number of victory points is given.

A Ship Panel is given listing the ships belonging to the empire. This may be blank if it is not your empire and Allow Omniscent Sensors is turned off.

Lastly a World Panel lists the worlds that belong to this Pocket Empire.
