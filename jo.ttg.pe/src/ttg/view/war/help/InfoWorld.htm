World Information Panel

The World Information Panel gives you information on the status of a system. At the top the name of the world and it's icon are given. Beneath is the Universal Planetary Profile, the Pocket Empire to which is belongs and it's coat of arms.

The population digit is given, as well as the total number of people. The fuel field lists the number and types of refueling points in the system. If the text is red, then the fueling points are underdefended, and opponent ships can refuel here without combat.

Repairs indicates how frequently this world can repair ships.

For worlds that can only repair a certain number per game it will also indicate how many reapirs are left.

The resources field tells you how many resources are generated per turn. Points indicates the number of victory points this world is worth.

A Ship Panel is given listing the known ships in the system. This may be blank if this world does not belong to you and Allow Omniscent Sensors is turned off.

Lastly there may be a starport button. This will take you to the Starport Information Panel if you can build ships on this world.
