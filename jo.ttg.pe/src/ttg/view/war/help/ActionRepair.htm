Repair Ships Action Panel

Ships may become damaged in combat. If they wait for a turn without moving or targeting another ship in combat they become eligable to be repaired. Starports can repair ships at varying rates.
PortCan Repair
ATwo per turn
BOne per turn
CTwo per game
DOne per game

To repair a ship, select one or more ships from the Ship Panel. Click "Set" and the ships will be marked for repair. When a ship has been assigned, the "to do" arrow will be filled in.

At any point you can click "Reset" to unassign all ships.

When you have assigned all ships you can click the "Done" button to move on. You will be notifed that the ships have been repaired.
